Brief Description of Our App.

In every relationship, personal or professional, there will always be some disagreement. You will never find an environment where people always agree and that understand each other. That's fantasy, not reality.

Let's Reconcile is a reconciliation platform for everyone and our mandate is to promote the spirit of reconciliation among friends, families, acquaintance, neighbours, business partners, communities and in fact the whole Nation!

In the current century many people diagree on different things and Ideologies but reconciliation hasn't been centres has not been set to help solve the situation. Through Our App we wish to use it as a tool for reconciling individuals.

Our Vision
